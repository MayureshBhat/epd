## 🎯 5-Minute Deployment Path

Print this and follow along! ✅

---

## STEP 1: GITHUB SETUP (2 minutes)

### ☐ Create GitHub Account
- Go to: github.com
- Click "Sign up"
- Fill in: Email, Password, Username
- Verify email
- ✅ Done!

### ☐ Create Repository
- Click "+" → "New repository"
- Name it: `signbridge-app`
- Select: **PUBLIC** ⭐
- Check: "Add README.md"
- Click: "Create repository"
- ✅ Done!

### ☐ Upload Files
- Click: "Add file" → "Upload files"
- Drag & drop:
  - `app_streamlit.py`
  - `requirements-streamlit.txt`
- Commit with message: "Add app"
- ✅ Done!

---

## STEP 2: STREAMLIT CLOUD (3 minutes)

### ☐ Create Streamlit Account
- Go to: share.streamlit.io
- Click: "Sign in with GitHub"
- Authorize Streamlit
- ✅ Done!

### ☐ Deploy App
- Click: "New app" (top left)
- **GitHub account:** YOUR-USERNAME ✅
- **Repository:** signbridge-app ✅
- **Branch:** main ✅
- **Main file path:** app_streamlit.py ✅
- Click: "Deploy!"
- ✅ Wait 2-3 minutes...

### ☐ App is Live!
- ✅ Deployment successful!
- Get your URL: https://signbridge-app.streamlit.app
- ✅ Done!

---

## STEP 3: TEST & SHARE (1 minute)

### ☐ Test App
- Click your URL
- Click camera button
- Make ASL hand sign
- See detected letter ✅
- All working? Great!

### ☐ Share with Professor
- Copy your URL
- Send to professor
- Professor opens link
- Professor impressed! 🎓
